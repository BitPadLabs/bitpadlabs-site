## About Miro

Miro is the Innovation Workspace that enables distributed teams to collaborate effectively, from brainstorming to planning and beyond. With its intuitive online whiteboard platform, Miro helps teams:

- **Brainstorm and ideate** with infinite canvas and real-time collaboration
- **Plan and organize** projects with visual frameworks and templates
- **Design and prototype** using integrated design thinking tools
- **Facilitate workshops** with interactive activities and voting features
- **Connect workflows** through 100+ integrations with tools like Jira, Slack, and Microsoft Teams
