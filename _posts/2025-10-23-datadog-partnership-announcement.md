We're thrilled to announce that **BitPadLabs** has been accepted into the **Datadog Partner Network (DPN)** as a certified **Solution Provider**!

This partnership enables us to deliver comprehensive observability and monitoring solutions to our government and commercial clients, helping organizations gain real-time visibility into their infrastructure, applications, and security posture. ## About Datadog

Datadog is the monitoring and security platform for cloud applications. Organizations of all sizes across various industries use Datadog to enable digital transformation and cloud migration, drive collaboration among development, operations, security and business teams, accelerate time to market, reduce time to problem resolution, secure applications and infrastructure, understand user behavior, and track key business metrics.

Datadog provides:

- **Infrastructure Monitoring:** Real-time visibility across servers, containers, databases, and cloud services
- **Application Performance Monitoring (APM):** End-to-end distributed tracing and performance insights
- **Log Management:** Centralized log aggregation, search, and analytics at scale
- **Security Monitoring:** Threat detection, compliance tracking, and security analytics
- **Network Performance Monitoring:** Deep network visibility and troubleshooting
- **Real User Monitoring (RUM):** Frontend performance and user experience tracking
