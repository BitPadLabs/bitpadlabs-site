Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about BitPadLabs and our services

About BitPadLabs

What is BitPadLabs?

+

BitPadLabs is an independent software development lab founded in Mississippi. We focus on creating clean, scalable software solutions that solve real problems for real people. Our company was founded by Christopher Inman and Hannah Cassels with a mission to blend technology with human-centered design.

Where is BitPadLabs located?

+

BitPadLabs, LLC is a Mississippi-based company. While we operate primarily as a remote team, our company is legally registered in Mississippi.

What does your company name mean?

+

The name "BitPadLabs" combines "Bit" (representing digital technology) and "Pad" (a comfortable space), creating the concept of a comfortable digital workspace where technology thrives. The "Labs" portion emphasizes our commitment to innovation and experimentation.

Our Services

What services does BitPadLabs offer?

+

BitPadLabs provides custom software development, technology consulting, AI integration, mobile-first development, and DevOps automation. We specialize in creating scalable, privacy-first solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

What makes BitPadLabs different?

+

As a Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB), we bring diverse perspectives and innovative thinking to every project. We prioritize clean, maintainable code, human-centered design, and strategic partnerships with industry leaders like Miro and Red Hat.

Do you work with government contracts?

+

Yes! BitPadLabs is a certified Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB) registered with SAM.gov. We have the necessary certifications and registrations to work on federal contracts, including our DUNS, UEI, and CAGE codes.

Collaboration & Support

How can I contribute to your projects?

+

We welcome collaboration through our GitHub repositories. You can submit issues, pull requests, or join discussions on open-source projects. For partnership opportunities or custom development inquiries, contact us at info@bitpadlabs.com.

How can I get support or consultation?

+

For consulting services, partnership inquiries, or project discussions, contact us at info@bitpadlabs.com. We offer initial consultations to understand your needs and determine how we can best support your technology goals.

Do you offer custom software development services?

+

Yes! Custom software development is one of our core services. We work closely with clients to design and build scalable, maintainable solutions tailored to their specific needs. Contact us at info@bitpadlabs.com to discuss your project.
