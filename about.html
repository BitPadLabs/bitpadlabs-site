About BitPadLabs

Our mission, values, and approach

Certified Woman-Owned Small Business

As of January 12, 2026

Our Mission

BitPadLabs delivers innovative technology solutions through strategic partnerships and expert software development. As a certified Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB), we're committed to helping organizations solve real problems through clean, scalable technology, while supporting diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.

Our Story

BitPadLabs is a certified Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB) founded by Hannah Cassels (CEO), Jaime Inman (Chief Operating & Financial Officer), and Melanie Amerson (Chief Strategy Officer), who collectively serve as the company's owners and founding members. Christopher Inman (CTO) and Ryan Burke (CGO) join them as co-founders, bringing deep expertise in technology leadership, federal modernization, and business development.

Operating in Georgia and Mississippi, BitPadLabs is focused on delivering practical, high-quality software solutions, strategic technology consulting, and innovative partnerships with industry leaders like Miro and Red Hat. We serve commercial and government clients, leveraging our WOSB certification to support diversity initiatives across the public and private sectors.

WOSB Certification

Our Woman-Owned Small Business certification, awarded January 12, 2026, represents our commitment to diversity in technology and opens opportunities for federal contracting through the WOSB Federal Contracting Program. We are registered in SAM.gov with:

  • DUNS: 144845731
  • UEI: WXU7TJ5MQJW4
  • CAGE: 15LW1

Our Approach

We believe in human-centered design, privacy-first development, and sustainable business practices. Through strategic partnerships with industry leaders and deep technical expertise, we deliver solutions that create lasting value and meaningful impact for our clients.

Core Principles

  • Strategic Vision over Short-Term Gains — We think in decades and execute in sprints.
  • Partnership-Driven Innovation — Collaborating with industry leaders to amplify our capabilities.
  • Solve Real Problems with Tech — No vanity projects. No tech theater.
  • Lead with Data, Design with Empathy — We build with users, not just for them.
  • Quality and Security First — Clean code, comprehensive testing, and privacy-first design.
  • Diversity as Strength — Woman-owned leadership bringing diverse perspectives to every project.
