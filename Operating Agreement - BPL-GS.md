# **OPERATING AGREEMENT**

## **BITPADLABS GOVERNMENT SOLUTIONS LLC**

This Operating Agreement (the “Agreement”) is entered into as of \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_, 2026, by and among the Members of **BitPadLabs Government Solutions LLC**, a limited liability company to be formed under the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia (the “Company”).

This Agreement is intended to establish clear ownership, management, and control consistent with Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) requirements and SBA VetCert eligibility.

---

## **1\. FORMATION**

### **1.1 Formation**

The Company shall be formed as a limited liability company under the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia upon the filing of Articles of Organization with the Virginia State Corporation Commission.

### **1.2 Name**

The name of the Company is **BitPadLabs Government Solutions LLC**.

### **1.3 Principal Place of Business**

The principal place of business of the Company shall be the residence of the Managing Member, as reflected in the Company’s formation documents and SAM.gov registration, and may be changed from time to time by the Managing Member in accordance with applicable law.

---

## **2\. PURPOSE**

### **2.1 Business Purpose**

The purpose of the Company is to provide technology, engineering, modernization, DevSecOps, and related professional services to federal, state, and local government customers, including but not limited to the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) and state-level veteran-related programs.

### **2.2 SDVOSB Intent**

The Company is structured and operated to qualify as a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) under applicable SBA rules and regulations. All provisions of this Agreement shall be interpreted to preserve that status.

---

### **3.2 Unconditional Ownership**

---

## **4\. MANAGEMENT AND CONTROL**

### **4.1 Manager-Managed LLC**

The Company shall be a manager-managed limited liability company.

### **4.2 Managing Member**

Ryan Burke is hereby designated as the **Managing Member** of the Company.

### **4.3 Authority of Managing Member**

The Managing Member shall have exclusive and final authority over the management and daily business operations of the Company, including but not limited to:

* Entering into, performing, and terminating contracts

* Hiring, supervising, and terminating personnel

* Financial management, banking, and budgeting decisions

* Strategic direction and business planning

* Execution of teaming agreements, subcontracts, and intercompany services agreements

* Execution of all documents binding the Company

The Managing Member devotes and shall continue to devote sufficient time and attention to the management and daily operations of the Company to ensure independent control consistent with SDVOSB requirements.

### **4.4 Non-Managing Member Role**

Christopher Inman shall have no authority to bind the Company except as expressly delegated in writing by the Managing Member.

### **4.5 No Shared or Negative Control**

No provision of this Agreement shall be interpreted to require supermajority approval, mutual consent, veto rights, quorum requirements, or other mechanisms that would limit, condition, or impair the Managing Member’s unconditional control over the Company.

## **6\. CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS**

### **6.1 Initial Contributions**

Initial capital contributions, if any, shall be recorded in the Company’s books.

### **6.2 No Mandatory Additional Contributions**

No Member shall be required to make additional capital contributions unless approved by the Managing Member.

---

## **7\. PROFITS, LOSSES, AND DISTRIBUTIONS**

### **7.1 Allocations**

Profits and losses shall be allocated in proportion to ownership interests unless otherwise required by law.

### **7.2 Distributions**

Distributions of available cash shall be made at the discretion of the Managing Member and in proportion to ownership interests, subject to applicable law. No Member or non-member officer is entitled to guaranteed distributions.

### **7.3 Officer Compensation**

The Managing Member may approve compensation for Members and/or officers for services actually rendered to the Company. Any such compensation shall:

* Be discretionary and approved in writing by the Managing Member

* Be market-reasonable for the services performed

* Not be guaranteed

* Not be tied to ownership percentage or voting rights

* Not confer additional ownership, control, or profit-sharing rights

The Members acknowledge that, at the initial operating stage, the Managing Member intends to allocate a modest base level of compensation for the CEO and CTO, anticipated to be up to ten percent (10%) of Company gross receipts in the aggregate, subject to adjustment by the Managing Member based on Company needs, cash flow, and compliance considerations.

Nothing in this section obligates the Company to pay compensation in any period, nor limits the Managing Member’s authority to increase, decrease, or suspend such compensation.

---

## **8\. BANKING AND ACCOUNTING**

### **8.1 Banking**

The Company shall maintain financial records sufficient to clearly attribute revenues and expenses to the Company. Banking arrangements shall be determined by the Managing Member.

### **8.2 Intercompany Services**

The Company may procure services from affiliated entities, including BitPadLabs LLC, under written agreements at market-reasonable rates. Such arrangements shall not impair the Managing Member’s control or the Company’s eligibility as a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business.

---

## **9\. TRANSFERS AND RESTRICTIONS**

### **9.1 Transfer Restrictions**

No Member may transfer any ownership interest without the prior written consent of the Managing Member.

### **9.2 SDVOSB Protection**

Any transfer or action that would jeopardize SDVOSB eligibility is prohibited.

---

## **10\. DISSOLUTION**

### **10.1 Events of Dissolution**

The Company may be dissolved upon the decision of the Managing Member or as required by law.

### **10.2 Winding Up**

The Managing Member shall oversee winding up and liquidation.

---

## **11\. MISCELLANEOUS**

### **11.1 Amendments**

This Agreement may be amended only with the written approval of the Managing Member.

### **11.2 Governing Law**

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia.

### **11.3 Severability**

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain in effect.
