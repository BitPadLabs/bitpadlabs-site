Miro

Visual Collaboration Platform

Partnership Overview

BitPadLabs partners with Miro to bring world-class visual collaboration capabilities to our clients. Miro's intuitive online whiteboard platform enables distributed teams to brainstorm, plan, design, and collaborate in real-time, transforming how organizations work together.

What We Deliver

  • Miro Implementation & Training: Custom onboarding, template development, and team training to maximize adoption and productivity
  • Workflow Integration: Connecting Miro with your existing tools (Jira, Slack, Microsoft Teams, etc.) for seamless collaboration
  • Design Thinking Facilitation: Leading interactive workshops and design sprints using Miro as the collaborative canvas
  • Custom Template Development: Building organization-specific Miro templates for recurring workflows, meetings, and processes
  • Agile & SAFe Enablement: Using Miro boards for PI Planning, retrospectives, roadmapping, and Agile ceremonies
  • Remote Collaboration Strategy: Designing effective remote and hybrid work practices powered by Miro's visual workspace

Red Hat

Enterprise Open Source Solutions

Partnership Overview

BitPadLabs collaborates with Red Hat to bring enterprise-grade open source technologies and cloud-native solutions to our clients. Red Hat's proven platforms for containers, Kubernetes, automation, and hybrid cloud enable organizations to innovate faster and operate more efficiently.

Technology Areas

  • Red Hat OpenShift: Enterprise Kubernetes platform for containerized applications and cloud-native development
  • Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL): Stable, secure, and certified operating system for mission-critical workloads
  • Red Hat Ansible Automation: IT automation for configuration management, application deployment, and orchestration
  • Hybrid Cloud Solutions: Consistent operations across on-premises, private cloud, and public cloud environments
  • DevOps & CI/CD: Modern development workflows with container-native tools and automated pipelines
  • Middleware & Integration: Application runtime, messaging, and integration platforms for enterprise applications
